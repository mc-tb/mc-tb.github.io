Meridian Concepts:
Over 30 Years of Expertise in Distressed Properties

Since the early 1990s, Meridian Concepts has specialized in construction project management and development, focusing on distressed real estate projects with various challenges.

Our portfolio includes projects ranging from $20 million to $100 million in asset value. These projects often faced significant issues, including cost overruns, construction losses, delays in sales and leasing, and difficulties in meeting debt service obligations.

Our Mission and Approach

At Meridian, our primary mission is to identify and resolve the critical challenges of distressed projects. When engaged, we conduct a comprehensive audit to assess the project’s condition and implement tailored solutions. This includes:

  • Eliminating Cost Overruns: Identifying inefficiencies and re-aligning budgets to control expenses.
  • Injecting Capital: Providing or facilitating necessary funding to address immediate financial needs.
  • Meeting Milestones: Accelerating construction progress to achieve critical goals, including obtaining certificates of occupancy.
  • Enhancing Marketing and Leasing: Collaborating with marketing teams to drive property sales and increase tenant occupancy.
  • Facilitating Debt Service: Structuring solutions to ensure debt obligations are met.

Our Clients

Our clients include banks, financial institutions, and investment groups—the capital providers behind these distressed assets. Serving as their owner’s representative, we take full management control to complete the project and protect their investments.

From construction oversight to marketing and leasing strategies, we provide the expertise needed to stabilize properties, secure tenants, and maximize asset value.

Industry Connections

Our extensive network within the distressed real estate market provides us with access to performing and non-performing notes from the real estate divisions of banks. These connections allow us to efficiently match buyers and sellers and facilitate successful note transactions.

Our Services

At Meridian Concepts, we provide a comprehensive range of services tailored to address the unique challenges of distressed real estate projects and investment portfolios:

1. Distressed Note Brokering (Buy & Sell)
Facilitating the purchase and sale of distressed notes, connecting buyers and sellers to maximize returns and resolve distressed asset situations efficiently.

2. Construction Project Management & Development
Offering end-to-end construction management and development solutions, ensuring projects are completed on time, within budget, and aligned withstrategic goals.

3. Project Management Audit Services
Conducting detailed audits of ongoing or stalled projects to identify inefficiencies, address challenges, and implement corrective measures.

4. Bank Management for Distressed Properties
Managing distressed properties on behalf of banks, ensuring assets are stabilized, value is retained, and financial objectives are met.

5. Capital Call & Loan Restructuring for Commercial Properties Under Construction
Assisting with capital injection and loan restructuring to keep under-construction commercial properties on track and financially viable.

6. Bank Portfolio & Investment Project Management
Providing expert guidance and management for bank portfolios and various investment projects, ensuring optimal performance and value creation.
